Together, we build our generation's minds.

We are proud to announce the first summit for junior programmers in Egypt, and we are happy to invite all our junior talents all around Egypt to attend our summit, and have the chance to win the big prize.This Summit is a biennial event hosted by a group of the best Programming schools in Egypt. In 2022, it will be stream live on all sponsers social media pages. The theme for summit 2022 is “Together, we build our generation's minds“.

2022.11.24(THU) ~ 28(MON)

@ Egypt Expo & convention Authority CONVENTIONS HALL, Nasr City.
